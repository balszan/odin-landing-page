This goose honks so hard, it's unbelievable. Some people consider geese to be aggressive, but this is not true. They are simply assertive!

Look at these geese.

The Egyptian goose is an African member of the duck family Anatidae. Because of their popularity chiefly as an ornamental bird, the species has also been introduced to Europe, United States and elsewhere outside their natural range. This one is my favourite!

This one is not my favourite, but look at that silly forehead. Martha Steward owns these, if you believe her blog! The Chinese goose is an international breed of domestic goose, known by this name in Europe and in North America.

The Canada goose is one of the largest members of the waterfowl family. The subspecies that breeds in Indiana is the giant Canada goose (Branta canadensis maxima). These geese were common birds throughout the Midwest before European settlement. Very loud honks!

The friendliest goose! The Toulouse is a French breed of large domestic goose, originally from the area of Toulouse in south-western France.

Geese are highly social animals. If they are raised around other livestock and fowl, they usually get along well with them. Goose is actually the term for female geese, male geese are called ganders. A group of geese on land or in water are a gaggle, while in the air they are called a skein.
